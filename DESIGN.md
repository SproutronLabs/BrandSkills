# Rebel Cheese — Brand Design System

## Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Rebel Pink | `#F2E0DA` | Primary background, hero sections |
| Parchment | `#F9F3F2` | Secondary background, email body |
| Coral | `#E9726D` | CTAs, accents, urgency |
| Teal | `#537E7C` | Secondary CTAs, trust elements |
| Dusty Rose | `#D7A89B` | Soft accents, dividers |
| Terracotta | `#9A5C4C` | Headings, warm emphasis |

**Rules:**
- Primary backgrounds: Rebel Pink or Parchment (never white)
- CTAs: Coral on Parchment, or Teal on Pink
- Text on dark: Parchment (#F9F3F2)
- Never use pure black (#000) — use dark Terracotta or #2A2A2A

## Typography

| Role | Font | Style |
|------|------|-------|
| Logo | Caligor Sans | Brand mark only |
| Headings (H1-H4) | Roca One | Warm, artisanal feel — use variants |
| Body / Paragraph | Inter | Clean, readable |
| Subheaders / Buttons | Work Sans | All caps for CTAs |

**Rules:**
- Headings always Roca One — never Inter or Work Sans
- Buttons: Work Sans, uppercase, letter-spacing 1-2px
- Body text: Inter, 14-16px, line-height 1.6
- Never use generic system fonts

## Brand Voice

**Personality:** An intelligent, well-traveled, confident woman who is passionate about innovation, sustainability, and the craft of cheesemaking. Warm, welcoming, collaborative. Hands-on — dives into the lab, not just theory.

**Tone:**
- Professional but fun — authority without being stiff
- Conversational — use contractions, natural language
- Educational but accessible — break complex topics into bite-sized insights
- Confident yet inclusive — we know our craft, but everyone is welcome
- Sophisticated yet inviting — quality without exclusivity

**Do:**
- Use "we" not "Rebel Cheese" in body copy
- Reference plant-based positively — "crafted from cashews" not "dairy-free alternative"
- Short sentences, conversational rhythm
- Emphasize artisanal craft, cheesemaking tradition
- "Guilt-free" is OK, "fake cheese" is never OK

**Don't:**
- Never defensive about being plant-based
- Never clinical or corporate tone
- Never "vegan cheese" as primary descriptor — use "artisanal plant-based cheese"
- Never discount-first messaging for Loyalists (save discounts for Win-Back)
- Never all-caps body text

## Image Style

**Aesthetic:** French-modern elegance with a touch of playfulness.

**Photography principles:**
- Warm, natural lighting — golden hour feel
- Surfaces: marble, rustic wood, linen — never sterile/clinical
- Cheese styled as artisanal, handcrafted — show texture, aging, craft
- Lifestyle: charcuterie boards, wine pairings, gatherings, kitchen scenes
- Hero product: Cave-Aged Brie — always feature prominently when relevant
- Color palette of photos should echo brand colors (warm pinks, terracotta, cream)

**Never:**
- Stock photo feel — every image should feel editorial
- Sterile white backgrounds
- Overhead flat-lay only — mix angles
- Crowded compositions — let the cheese breathe

## Email Layout

**Structure:**
- Hero image: full-width, 600x300px minimum
- Max 2 CTAs per email
- Primary CTA: Coral (#E9726D) button, Work Sans uppercase
- Secondary CTA: Teal (#537E7C) text link
- Background: Parchment (#F9F3F2) body, Rebel Pink (#F2E0DA) header
- Footer: minimal, warm sign-off ("With love, The Rebel Cheese Team")

**By campaign type:**

| Campaign | Hero Style | CTA Text | Tone |
|----------|-----------|----------|------|
| Win-Back | Their previously purchased product, warm "we miss you" | "Come Back to the Board" | Personal, warm nostalgia |
| Reorder Ready | Product they last ordered, fresh styling | "Time to Restock" | Gentle reminder, no discount |
| VIP Re-engagement | Exclusive/new product, premium feel | "Your Exclusive Taste" | VIP treatment, early access |
| Seasonal (Father's Day etc.) | Gift-styled, celebration context | "Give the Gift of Craft" | Celebratory, gifting |
| First Purchase | Hero product (Brie), full spread | "Start Your Journey" | Welcoming, discovery |
| New Product Launch | New product hero, close-up texture | "Be the First to Try" | Excitement, exclusivity |

## Mission Context

Rebel Cheese exists to revolutionize the perception of plant-based cheese by creating innovative, delicious products that rival traditional dairy in taste, texture, and quality. Artisanal cheeses designed for charcuterie boards and wine pairings — a true gourmet experience.

**Differentiator:** The artisanal plant-based cheese for food connoisseurs, wine lovers, and charcuterie enthusiasts.

**Primary audience:** Women 35-65, interested in gourmet plant-based alternatives without sacrificing taste or quality.
